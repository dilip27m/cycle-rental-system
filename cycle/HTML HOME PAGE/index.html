About CycleInstant

Our Story

CycleInstant is a motorcycle rental service that offers a wide variety of cycles, including off-road, on-road, urban, specialty, and simple cycles. We aim to cater to specific customer preferences and offer diverse options for various riding environments. Our unique selling point is the 'Instant Access' feature, allowing users to unlock and rent cycles instantly using their smartphones.

Since 2000, we have provided customers with the easiest and most convenient way to rent a motorcycle. Our rates are affordable and our service is impeccable, so browse our inventory and pick the one that's best for you.
