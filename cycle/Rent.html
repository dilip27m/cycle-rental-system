Process to rent a cycle

  • Login, if new user needs to register then you can able to rent a cycle.
  • Then rent page will update to rent a cycle.
  • Enter the details in the rent page.
  • Based on cycle type and time money will be calculated.
    • City cycle: 10 rupees per hour
    • Travel cycle: 15 rupees per hour
    • Gear cycle: 20 rupees per hour
  • You can enter coupon if you have, you will be getting a discount.
  • On clicking show fares it will display the final amount.
  • It will also display user details entered in the rent form.
  • There will be a unique token generated (token is checked by the cycle shop owner).
  • After completing the payment the details will be displayed on the dashboard.
  • Take a note of token. Don't share token with others.
  • If end time is crossed by the user, he needs to pay an extra amount per each hour: 15 rupees.
  • This amount will be collected by the cycle shop owner.
